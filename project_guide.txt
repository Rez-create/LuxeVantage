PROJECT NAME: LUXEVANTAGE

LuxeVantage is a luxury brand that deals with selling of luxurious automobiles, fashion and 
apparel(cloths), Jewellery and Watches.

HOW TO NAVIGATE THROUGH THE WEBSITE
- The main index.html file is below when you open the folder containing the project(LuxeVantage).
- To access the brands within LuxeVantage, navigate to services section and either click Know More 
  or tap on the image.
- The contributors to the project are in Directors section.
- To access each directors profile, click About me button below the respective picture.
